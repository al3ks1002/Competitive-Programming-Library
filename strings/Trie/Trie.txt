You have an empty list of words.
You are given M (<= 10^5) operations:
0 w - add an apparition of word w in the list;
1 w - delete an apparition of word w from the list;
2 w - print the number of apparitions of word w;
3 w - print the largest prefix of word w with any word from the list;

w is between 1 and 20 characters (lowercase english letters).
